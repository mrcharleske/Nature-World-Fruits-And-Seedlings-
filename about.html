Our Story

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Makuyu, Kenya, Nature World Fruits Seedlings began as a small family nursery with a big dream: to empower Kenyan farmers with the highest quality seedlings and expert agricultural knowledge.

What started as a modest operation has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted seedling suppliers, serving over 10,000 satisfied customers across the country. Our founder, Joseph Kinyuti, combined his passion for agriculture with years of hands-on farming experience to create a business that truly understands the needs of Kenyan farmers.

Today, we're proud to offer over 50 varieties of premium seedlings, from traditional favorites like mangoes and avocados to exotic options like dragon fruit and vanilla. Every seedling that leaves our nursery carries with it our commitment to quality, sustainability, and your success.
